=== SLIDE 14 ===
Decision-making
 
Unanimous: 
Everyone is in complete agreement.
Consensus: 
May not be the first preference of individuals in a group but it is a decision to which everyone can give their consent. Good consensus process relies heavily on problem-solving, questioning, empathy, and self-sacrifice.
Stalemate: 
An even draw in terms of those in 
favour
 and those opposed. Further discussion is required to move forward.
