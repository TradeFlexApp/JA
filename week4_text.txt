=== SLIDE 43 ===
Personal Investments 
Bootstrapping
Mailchimp, an email marketing platform, was founded in 2001. The founders bootstrapped the business for years without any venture capital. 
By keeping the company profitable from the start and focusing on customers, Mailchimp grew into a highly successful company, valued at $12 billion when it was acquired by Intuit in 2021.
